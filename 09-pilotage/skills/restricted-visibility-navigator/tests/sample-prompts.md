# Sample Test Prompts for restricted-visibility-navigator

## Test 1: Fog Encounter
**Prompt**: "Visibility just dropped to 0.5nm. We're sailing at 5 knots. What should I do?"
**Expected**: List immediate actions (reduce speed, sound signals, post lookout, lights on), provide sound signal for sailing vessel, recommend navigation strategy.

## Test 2: Sound Signal Identification
**Prompt**: "I hear one long blast every 2 minutes. What vessel is that?"
**Expected**: Identify power vessel making way, explain significance, recommend actions (monitor radar, keep clear).

## Test 3: Decision to Anchor
**Prompt**: "In fog, visibility 0.2nm, depth 8m, 2nm from harbor entrance. Continue or anchor?"
**Expected**: Recommend anchoring (very poor visibility, safe depth, close to hazards), provide anchoring procedure and sound signal (bell every minute).
