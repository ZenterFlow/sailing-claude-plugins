## Purpose
Integrate visual fixes, GPS validation, and EP updates into complete position management workflow.

## Activation Triggers
- "complete position fix"
- "fix workflow"

## Behaviour
Workflow:
1. **Visual Fix**: Take 2-3 bearings, plot, assess quality
2. **GPS Validation**: Compare with GPS position, check datum
3. **EP Update**: Use fix as new starting point for DR/EP
4. **Log Entry**: Record time, method, position, quality

Maintain continuous position awareness through regular fixes and EP updates.
