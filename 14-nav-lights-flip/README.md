## üéØ Skills (3)

### 1. **lights-flashcard-quiz**
Interactive quiz testing recognition of navigation light configurations for all vessel types under COLREGS Rules 20-31.

**Coverage**:
- Power-driven vessels (all length categories)
- Sailing vessels and motor-sailing
- Fishing vessels (trawling vs other fishing)
- Special status vessels (NUC, RAM, CBD)
- Pilot vessels, towing, and pushing configurations
- Vessels at anchor and aground

**Key Exam Points**:
- Motor-sailing = forward masthead light mandatory
- NUC (two red vertical) vs RAM (red-white-red vertical)
- CBD (three red vertical) uses power-driven fog signals
- Over 50m vessels have TWO masthead lights (aft higher)

### 2. **shapes-identifier**
Day shapes identification quiz covering all COLREGS day signals displayed during daylight.

**Coverage**:
- Ball (anchor), two balls (NUC), ball-diamond-ball (RAM)
- Cone apex down (motor-sailing)
- Cylinder (CBD)
- Two cones apexes together (trawling) vs apart (other fishing)
- Diamond (towing)
- Three balls vertical (aground)
- Special cases (minesweeping, diving operations)

**Critical Details**:
- All shapes must be black, minimum 0.6m diameter
- Cone apex DOWN for motor-sailing (not up)
- Three balls = aground, one ball = anchor

### 3. **sound-signal-quiz**
Quiz on fog signals, maneuvering signals, warning signals, and anchor signals.

**Coverage**:
- Fog signals (every 2 minutes): power making way, stopped, sailing/NUC/RAM
- Anchor signals (every 1 minute): bell, gong for >100m, aground pattern
- Maneuvering signals: course alteration (1/2/3 short blasts)
- Overtaking signals in narrow channels
- Danger/doubt signal (5+ short rapid blasts)

**Critical Intervals**:
- Underway fog signals: Every 2 minutes
- Anchor signals: Every 1 minute
- CBD uses power-driven fog signals (NOT NUC/RAM pattern)

## üîë Critical Exam Distinctions

### Most Commonly Confused
1. **Motor-Sailing**: Must show masthead light + cone apex down
2. **NUC vs RAM**: Two red (NUC) vs red-white-red (RAM)
3. **CBD vs RAM**: Three red (CBD) + uses power signals, NOT RAM pattern
4. **Trawling vs Other Fishing**: Green-over-white (trawling) vs red-over-white (other)
5. **Anchor Intervals**: Every 1 minute (NOT 2 minutes)
6. **Aground vs Anchor**: Three balls (aground) vs one ball (anchor)
7. **Power Stopped vs Making Way**: Two prolonged (stopped) vs one prolonged (making way)

### Memory Aids Taught
- "Green over White, Trawling at Night"
- "White over Red, Pilot Ahead"
- "Three Reds High, Deep Draft Nigh"
- "Apex Down, Engine's On"
- "One Long Alone, Power's Tone"
- "Anchor Rings Every Single Minute"
