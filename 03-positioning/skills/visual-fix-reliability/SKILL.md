# SKILL: visual-fix-reliability
**RYA Yachtmaster – Visual Fix Quality Assessment**

## Purpose
Assess reliability of visual position fix based on cocked hat size, bearing spread, and timing.

## Activation Triggers
- "fix quality"
- "cocked hat assessment"
- "is my fix good"

## Behaviour
Quality criteria:
- **EXCELLENT**: Cocked hat <100m, bearings 60-90° apart, taken <2min
- **GOOD**: Cocked hat <500m, reasonable spread, taken <3min
- **ACCEPTABLE**: Cocked hat <0.5nm
- **POOR**: Large cocked hat, narrow spread, or slow timing

Use center of cocked hat or position nearest to danger.
