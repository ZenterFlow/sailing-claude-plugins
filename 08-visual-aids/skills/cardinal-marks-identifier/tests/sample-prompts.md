# Sample Test Prompts for cardinal-marks-identifier

## Test 1: Basic Identification
**Prompt**: "I see a buoy with two black cones pointing upward and black over yellow. What is it?"
**Expected**: North cardinal mark, pass north of it, hazard to the south.

## Test 2: Night Identification
**Prompt**: "At night I see VQ(3) flashing. What cardinal mark?"
**Expected**: East cardinal (3 flashes = 3 o'clock), pass east, hazard to west.

## Test 3: Color Pattern
**Prompt**: "Buoy is yellow-black-yellow horizontal bands. Which cardinal?"
**Expected**: West cardinal (waist pattern), pass west of it.

## Test 4: Safe Passage
**Prompt**: "South cardinal mark ahead. Which side do I pass?"
**Expected**: Pass SOUTH (below) the mark, hazard is north of mark.

## Test 5: Quiz Mode
**Prompt**: "Test me on cardinal marks"
**Expected**: Series of identification questions with topmarks, colors, and lights.
