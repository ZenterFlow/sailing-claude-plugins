# SKILL: Nautical Almanac Information Extraction

## Description
Extract harbor-specific information from nautical almanacs including VHF channels, services, and regulatory details.

## Prerequisites
- Chart symbol recognition (SKILL_15)
- Basic harbor familiarization

## Learning Objectives
- Locate harbor entry information in almanac
- Identify appropriate VHF channels for marinas and port authorities
- List 5+ types of information available in almanacs
- Determine monitoring requirements for port entry

## Information Types
**Communications:**
- Marina contact channels (e.g., Setter Marina)
- Port authority monitoring channels
- Working channels vs calling channels

**Harbor Data:**
- Entry procedures and regulations
- Facilities and services
- Tidal information
- Pilotage requirements
- Customs and immigration

**Planning Use:**
- Harbor layout and berthing
- Local regulations
- Emergency contacts

## Common Errors
- Using calling channel for working communications
- Monitoring wrong VHF channels in port
- Relying on single source for safety planning

## Assessment
- Correctly answer 5 almanac lookup questions in &lt;5 minutes
- Explain why multiple publications are needed for safe passage planning
- Demonstrate channel selection for mock harbor entry scenario
