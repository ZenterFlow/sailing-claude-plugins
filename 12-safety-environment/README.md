## ðŸ”‘ Critical Safety Rules

1. **Life Jacket Crotch Strap**: Critical - prevents ride-up in water
2. **Dinghy Alcohol**: Zero tolerance - NO alcohol when operating
3. **EPIRB vs PLB**: EPIRB = vessel, PLB = person (not transferable)
4. **Fog First 2 Minutes**: Position, life jackets, reflector, lights, safe speed, signal
5. **Radar Reflector**: GRP/wood/carbon MUST have reflector (no natural reflection)
6. **Drogue Scope**: Sea anchor 10:1, stern drogue 5:1
7. **Safe Speed in Fog**: Stop within half visible distance
