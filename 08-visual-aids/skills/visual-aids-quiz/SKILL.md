## Behavior

### Systematic Identification Process

Use this 3-step process for instant, accurate mark identification:

**Step 1: Determine Color Scheme**
- **Red/Green**: Lateral marks (check IALA region)
- **Black/Yellow**: Cardinal marks
- **Black/Red Bands**: Isolated danger
- **Red/White Stripes**: Safe water
- **Yellow**: Special mark
- **Blue/Yellow**: Wreck marker

**Step 2: Identify Shape/Topmark**
- **Can (flat top)**: Port lateral
- **Cone (pointed)**: Starboard lateral
- **Two Cones**: Cardinal (direction indicates type)
- **Two Balls**: Isolated danger
- **Sphere**: Safe water
- **Cross (X)**: Special mark

**Step 3: Interpret Light Sequence**
- **2+1 Pattern**: Preferred channel mark
- **Fl(2)**: Isolated danger (white)
- **Fl(4)Y**: Special mark (yellow)
- **Quick Flashes**: Cardinal (3, 6, or 9)
- **Morse A**: Safe water
- **Alt Y/B**: Wreck marker

### Flash Count Mnemonics

**Cardinal Marks**: Remember "3-6-9"
- **East**: 3 flashes (Q(3) or VQ(3))
- **South**: 6 flashes + long flash (Q(6)+LFl)
- **West**: 9 flashes (Q(9) or VQ(9))
- **North**: Continuous quick (Q or VQ)

**Other Marks**:
- **Preferred Channel**: 2+1 pattern (unique identifier)
- **Isolated Danger**: 2 flashes (Fl(2))
- **Special Mark**: Often 4 flashes, but varies

### Cardinal Mark Details

**North Cardinal**:
- Colors: Black top, yellow bottom
- Topmark: Two cones pointing UP (↑↑)
- Mnemonic: "Points to north"
- Light: Continuous quick or very quick
- Pass: NORTH of mark

**East Cardinal**:
- Colors: Black-Yellow-Black (BYB) horizontal bands
- Topmark: Two cones base-to-base (◄►)
- Mnemonic: "Egg shape in middle"
- Light: Q(3) every 5/10s or VQ(3) every 5s
- Pass: EAST of mark

**South Cardinal**:
- Colors: Yellow top, black bottom
- Topmark: Two cones pointing DOWN (↓↓)
- Mnemonic: "Points to south"
- Light: Q(6)+LFl every 10/15s (6 quick + 1 long)
- Pass: SOUTH of mark

**West Cardinal**:
- Colors: Yellow-Black-Yellow (YBY) horizontal bands
- Topmark: Two cones point-to-point (►◄)
- Mnemonic: "Wasp waist / Wine glass"
- Light: Q(9) every 10/15s or VQ(9) every 10s
- Pass: WEST of mark

### Light Characteristics Quick Reference

Match these light patterns to mark types:

- **Iso.4s**: Safe water mark (isophase)
- **VQ(3)5s**: East cardinal (3 very quick flashes)
- **Fl.G.7s**: Starboard lateral (IALA A), any rhythm
- **Fl(2)10s**: White = isolated danger
- **Q**: North cardinal (continuous quick)
- **Fl.Y.5s**: Special mark (yellow)
- **Q.R**: Port lateral quick red (IALA A)
- **Q(9)10s**: West cardinal (9 quick flashes)
- **Q(6)+LFl.15s**: South cardinal (6 quick + 1 long)
- **Fl(2+1)G**: Preferred channel (2+1 pattern)

### Memory Aids

**Cardinal Topmarks**:
- **North**: Cones UP - "points to north"
- **South**: Cones DOWN - "points to south"
- **East**: Base to base - "egg in middle"
- **West**: Point to point - "wine glass / wasp waist"

**Cardinal Colors**:
- **North**: Black over yellow - "North is dark at top"
- **South**: Yellow over black - "South is light at top"
- **East/West**: Remember BYB = East, YBY = West

**Lateral Shapes**:
- **Can**: Flat top like a can of beans = PORT
- **Cone**: Pointed like ice cream cone = STARBOARD

**Regional Rules**:
- **IALA A**: "Red to port" (Europe, Australia)
- **IALA B**: "Green to port" (Americas, Asia)

### Common Errors to Avoid

❌ Confusing east and west cardinal color bands (BYB vs YBY)
❌ Mixing up 2+1 pattern meaning (indicates preferred channel, not isolated danger)
❌ Forgetting regional color reversal for lateral marks
❌ Confusing isolated danger (black/red) with wreck (blue/yellow)
❌ Miscounting quick flashes in cardinal sequence (3-6-9 pattern)
❌ Assuming any two-ball topmark is isolated danger (check colors!)
❌ Not checking IALA region before interpreting lateral marks
