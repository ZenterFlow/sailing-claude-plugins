# Sample Test Prompts for harbor-entry-planner

## Test 1: Basic Harbor Entry
**Prompt**: "Create entry plan for Fowey harbor. Vessel draught 1.5m, current conditions SW F3, HW-1."
**Expected**: Complete plan with approach, clearing bearings, abort criteria, Plans A/B/C.

## Test 2: Challenging Conditions
**Prompt**: "Entering Salcombe in poor visibility (0.5nm). Draught 2.0m, neap tide. Plan?"
**Expected**: Recognize marginal conditions, emphasize abort criteria, recommend delay or alternative.

## Test 3: First Time Entry
**Prompt**: "Never been to Plymouth before. Help me plan the approach."
**Expected**: Comprehensive plan emphasizing unfamiliarity, recommend daylight/good visibility, extra safety margins.
