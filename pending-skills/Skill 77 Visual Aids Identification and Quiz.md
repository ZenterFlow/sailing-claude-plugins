# SKILL: Visual Aids Identification and Quiz (SKILL_77)

## Description
Rapidly identify navigation marks from visual appearance, light characteristics, and chart symbols with 100% accuracy through systematic recognition techniques.

## Prerequisites
- Lateral Marks Identification (SKILL_70)
- Cardinal Marks Navigation (SKILL_71)
- Special Purpose Navigation Marks (SKILL_72)

## Learning Objectives
- Achieve instant recognition of all mark types
- Match light sequences to mark categories
- Identify marks from partial information
- Apply systematic identification process
- Score 100% on visual aids quizzes

**Systematic Identification Process:**

**Step 1: Determine Color Scheme**
- **Red/Green:** Lateral marks (check region)
- **Black/Yellow:** Cardinal marks
- **Black/Red Bands:** Isolated danger
- **Red/White Stripes:** Safe water
- **Yellow:** Special mark
- **Blue/Yellow:** Wreck marker

**Step 2: Identify Shape/Topmark**
- **Can:** Port lateral (or shape-appropriate special mark)
- **Cone:** Starboard lateral (or shape-appropriate special mark)
- **Two Cones:** Cardinal (direction indicates type)
- **Two Balls:** Isolated danger
- **Sphere:** Safe water
- **Cross (X):** Special mark

**Step 3: Interpret Light Sequence**
- **2+1 Pattern:** Preferred channel mark
- **Fl(2):** Isolated danger (white)
- **Fl(4)Y:** Special mark (yellow)
- **Quick Flashes:** Cardinal (3, 6, or 9)
- **Morse A:** Safe water
- **Alt Y/B:** Wreck marker

**Flash Count Mnemonics:**
- **Cardinal:** "3-6-9" (East-South-West)
- **North:** Continuous or quick (no number)
- **Preferred Channel:** 2+1 pattern (unique to this mark)
- **Isolated Danger:** 2 flashes

**Instant Recognition Guide:**

| Mark Type | Color | Topmark | Light Pattern | Passage Rule |
|-----------|-------|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Port Lateral (A) | Red | Can | Any except 2+1 | Leave to port |
| Starboard Lateral (A) | Green | Cone | Any except 2+1 | Leave to starboard |
| Preferred Channel | 2+1 stripes | Same as lateral | Fl(2+1) | Follow main channel |
| North Cardinal | Black/Yellow | Two cones up | Q/VQ/UQ | Pass north |
| South Cardinal | Yellow/Black | Two cones down | Q(6)+LFl | Pass south |
| East Cardinal | BYB | Two cones up/down | Q(3) | Pass east |
| West Cardinal | YBY | Two cones point/point | Q(9) | Pass west |
| Isolated Danger | Black/Red bands | Two balls | Fl(2) | Keep clear |
| Safe Water | R/W stripes | Sphere | Iso/Oc/Morse A | Safe passage |
| Special | Yellow | Cross (X) | Any Y sequence | Chart reference |
| Wreck | Blue/Yellow stripes | None | Alt Y/B | Keep clear |

**Common Quiz Questions:**
1. Port hand mark: Red can (A) or green can (B)
2. Isolated danger: Black with red bands, two ball topmark
3. Starboard hand mark: Green cone (A) or red cone (B)
4. Safe water: Red/white vertical stripes, spherical
5. Special mark: Yellow with X topmark
6. Easterly cardinal: BYB bands, cones up/down
7. Northerly cardinal: Black top/yellow bottom, cones up
8. Wreck buoy: Blue/yellow stripes, alternating Y/B light
9. Preferred channel to port: Two green, one red stripe, Fl(2+1)G
10. Westerly cardinal: YBY bands, cones point to point

**Light Characteristics Reference:**
- **Iso.4s:** Safe water mark
- **VQ(3)5s:** Easterly cardinal
- **Fl.G.7s:** Starboard lateral mark
- **Fl(2)10s:** Check if white (isolated danger) or other
- **Q.:** Northerly cardinal
- **Fl.Y.5s:** Special mark
- **Q.R.:** Quick red (port lateral)
- **Q(9)5s:** Westerly cardinal
- **Q(6)+LFl.15s:** Southerly cardinal

## Common Errors
- Confusing east and west cardinal color bands
- Mixing up 2+1 pattern meaning (indicates preferred channel)
- Forgetting regional color reversal for lateral marks
- Confusing isolated danger (black/red) with wreck (blue/yellow)
- Miscounting quick flashes (3-6-9 pattern)

## Assessment
- Identify mark from description alone
- Match light sequence to mark type
- Determine safe action from partial mark information
- Score 100% on visual identification quiz
- Troubleshoot identification error in real-time scenario
