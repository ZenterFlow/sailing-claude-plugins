# Light Characteristics Reference

## Basic Characteristics

**F - Fixed**
- Continuous steady light
- No flashes or eclipses
- Example: F R - Fixed red light

**Fl - Flashing**
- Flash < 2 seconds
- Dark period > light period
- Example: Fl W 5s - White flash every 5 seconds

- Dark period > light period
- Example: LFl 10s - Long flash every 10 seconds

**Oc - Occulting**
- Light period > dark period
- Brief eclipses
- Opposite of flashing
- Example: Oc 4s - Light eclipsed every 4 seconds

**Iso - Isophase**
- Light = dark
- Equal periods
- Example: Iso 6s - 3s on, 3s off

**Q - Quick**
- 50-60 flashes per minute
- Too fast to count
- Example: Q - Continuous quick flashing

**VQ - Very Quick**
- 100-120 flashes per minute
- Even faster
- Example: VQ - Continuous very quick

**UQ - Ultra Quick**
- 240-300 flashes per minute
- Extremely rapid
- Example: UQ - Ultra quick flashing

## Group Characteristics

**Fl(2)** - Two flashes in group
**Fl(3)** - Three flashes in group
**Oc(2)** - Two eclipses in group
**Q(3)** - Three quick flashes in group

## Composite Characteristics

**Fl(2+1)** - Group of 2 + group of 1
**Oc(2+3)** - Group of 2 eclipses + group of 3

## Colors

**W** - White (default if no color shown)
**R** - Red
**G** - Green
**Y** - Yellow
**Al WR** - Alternating white and red

## Reading Full Notation

**Example**: Fl(3) 15s 24m 18M

- **Fl(3)** - Flashing, 3 flashes per group
- **15s** - Period (complete cycle every 15 seconds)
- **24m** - Height above MHWS (24 meters)
- **18M** - Nominal range (18 nautical miles)
