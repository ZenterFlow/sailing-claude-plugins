## Time Calculations

**Lunar Day**: 24h 50min
- Tide times advance ~50 minutes per day
- Some calendar days have only 1 HW or 1 LW

**Period between tides** (semidiurnal):
- HW to LW: ~6h 12min
- LW to HW: ~6h 12min
- Total cycle: ~12h 25min
